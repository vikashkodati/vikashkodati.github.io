Rainbow Loom: Crafting and Conversations

One of our favorite at-home activities is creating Rainbow Loom designs. What started as a simple hobby has grown into a cherished bonding time where Nandini and I not only make beautiful pieces but also engage in deeper conversations. This creative outlet has brought us closer and has helped us both improve focus and patience.

Through these shared experiences, I am constantly reminded of how precious the time spent with Nandini is. Each adventure, whether big or small, helps build the foundation of love and trust within our family.
