Parenting Journey

Parenting has been one of the most fulfilling parts of my life. My daughter, Nandini, and I have formed a bond that continues to deepen as we spend time exploring, learning, and creating memories together. Whether it’s our regular visits to the Pacific Science Center with her classmates every couple of months or the simple joy of crafting with Rainbow Loom, every moment with her is invaluable.

Family Adventures

We love taking family trips to some of our favorite spots around Washington. Together with my wife, Saraswathi, and my mother, Parvathi, we enjoy picnics and outings at places like:

  • Seward Park
  • Golden Gardens Park
  • Ballard Locks
  • Snoqualmie Pass
  • Crescent Lake in the Olympic National Park

These adventures allow us to connect with nature, share stories, and bond as a family. Each place holds its own special memories, from serene moments by the water to exciting hikes through the mountains.

Rainbow Loom: Crafting Connections

One of Nandini's and my favorite activities is making Rainbow Loom creations. This fun, creative hobby has become a great way for us to engage in deeper conversations, improving our communication while also building patience and concentration. It’s these small moments that bring us closer together, strengthening our bond each day.

Being a parent is a constant journey of growth, and every day with Nandini teaches me something new. Our shared experiences remind me of the importance of love, patience, and presence in building strong, lasting family connections.
